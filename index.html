Blood Bank

Blood bank is a app for finding required group of blood in case of emergency.It is a platform between blood donor and blood receiver.A regular blood donor can create an account providing some information such as name,address,mobile no,blood group,etc.Anyone who needs blood can find a blood donor by filtering required option.

Why blood bank?

Everyday about **** people die in Bangladesh for not finding required blood in time. People depends on facebook post or tv advertisement for blood but a person with same group of blood at same place who is available to donate blood may not present at same time because of lack of communication based on facebook post only.It is Blood Bank which can solve this problem.
